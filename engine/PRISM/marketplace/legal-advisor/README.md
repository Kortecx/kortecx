# Legal Advisor

Contract review, compliance, privacy (GDPR/CCPA), terms of service, and IP protection expert.

## Capabilities

- Contract review with clause-by-clause risk analysis
- GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and cross-border data transfer assessment
- Intellectual property evaluation: copyright, trademark, patents, trade secrets
- Regulatory compliance mapping across SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI DSS, SOX
- Terms of service and privacy policy drafting and review

## Best Used For

- Reviewing vendor contracts and SaaS agreements
- Assessing data privacy compliance and drafting DPAs
- Evaluating open-source license obligations and IP ownership
- Creating terms of service, privacy policies, and acceptable use policies
- Risk assessment for new products, features, or market entry
